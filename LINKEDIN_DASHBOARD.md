# LinkedIn Growth Dashboard

A simple, self-contained dashboard to track your LinkedIn profile health, growth, and optimization.

## What It Does

✅ **Growth Tracking** - Monitor followers, connections, and profile views  
✅ **Message Management** - Track unread messages and pending connection requests  
✅ **Profile Optimization** - Checklist to optimize your LinkedIn profile  
✅ **Growth Trends** - Visual charts showing your growth over time  
✅ **Health Scoring** - Quick overview of your LinkedIn health  
✅ **Local Storage** - All data saved in your browser automatically  

## How to Use

1. **Open the dashboard**: Simply open `linkedin-dashboard.html` in any web browser
   - Double-click the file, or
   - Right-click → Open With → Browser

2. **Check your LinkedIn metrics** on your profile:
   - Go to **Profile** → **Analytics** (if available)
   - Check your **Followers**, **Profile Views**, and **Connections**
   - Count your **Unread Messages** in your inbox
   - Note **Pending Connection Requests**

3. **Enter the data** in the dashboard form fields and click **Update**

4. **Complete the optimization checklist** to improve your profile

5. **Check back regularly** - Daily or weekly for best tracking

## What Each Section Does

### 📈 Growth Metrics
- **Followers**: Total followers on your profile
- **Profile Views**: Monthly profile views (LinkedIn shows these in Analytics)
- **Connections**: Total connections
- **Post Engagement**: Average likes/comments on your recent posts

### 💬 Messages & Activity
- **Unread Messages**: Number of unread direct messages
- **Pending Requests**: Connection requests you haven't responded to yet
- **Last Check-in**: When you last reviewed your LinkedIn

### ✅ Profile Optimization
Complete these tasks to boost your visibility:
- Professional profile photo
- Keyword-rich headline
- Compelling About section with CTA
- Detailed work experience
- Top 3 skills endorsed
- Portfolio/website link
- "Open to opportunities" enabled
- Recent post (within 30 days)

### 📊 Growth Trend
Visual chart showing your followers, connections, and views over time

## Tips for Growth

**Post Consistently**
- 1-3 posts per week
- Share industry insights, tips, personal wins

**Engage Daily**
- Comment on 5-10 posts in your feed
- Send 5-10 personalized connection requests per week

**Optimize Your Profile**
- Use keywords relevant to your industry
- Include a clear call-to-action in your About
- Keep your profile photo recent and professional

**Respond Quickly**
- Reply to messages within 24 hours
- Accept relevant connection requests promptly

**Analyze & Adjust**
- Check your growth trends weekly
- Notice what posts get the most engagement
- Post more content like your top performers

## Data Storage

- All data is stored **locally in your browser**
- Nothing is sent to servers
- To back up: Use the **Export Data** button to download as CSV
- To clear: Use the **Reset** button (careful - can't undo!)

## Troubleshooting

**Can't see data I entered?**
- Make sure you clicked the **Update** button
- Check your browser's cookies/storage aren't blocked

**Numbers seem wrong?**
- LinkedIn doesn't always show exact numbers
- Round to nearest 5-10 if needed
- Consistent tracking matters more than perfect accuracy

**Want to use it on multiple devices?**
- Export your data on Device A (CSV file)
- There's no built-in import yet - you'd need to manually re-enter on Device B
- Consider keeping one "source of truth" device
